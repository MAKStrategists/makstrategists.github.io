Talent Pool (Optional Add-On)

Strategy is only as powerful as the people who execute it. For teams that need support finding the right execution talent, we offer an optional Talent Pool service.

What this is

We maintain a curated pool of junior and mid-level marketing and design professionals — people who can bring your strategy to life without the cost and overhead of a full agency.

Who you can find here

Junior marketers

For coordinating campaigns, managing channels, and implementing your marketing direction.

Designers

For decks, social visuals, basic brand assets, and presentation support.

Content support

Writers and content executors who can work from your strategic messaging to create aligned outputs.

How it works

  1. You share your needs, budget, and preferred working style.
  2. We share a short-list of profiles from our pool.
  3. You interview and choose who fits your team best.
  4. You engage them directly — we simply enable the match.

The Talent Pool is an optional add-on, designed to keep strategy and execution connected — without turning M.A.K Strategists into an agency.
